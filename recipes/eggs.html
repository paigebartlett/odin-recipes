No Stove Eggs

Image from Tengyart on Unsplash

Description

Want a fluffy fried egg but don't have a stove? No problem! All you need to make eggs with this recipe is a microwave! No skillet needed!

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • Butter or cooking oil
  • Fork
  • Microwave safe bowl and plate

Steps

  1. Grease the microwave safe bowl with the butter or cooking oil
  2. Crack the egg or eggs into the bowl
  3. Break the yolk or yolks with a fork
  4. Cover the bowl with the plate and place into the microwave
  5. Microwave the eggs in the bowl for one minute, then let rest for one minute
  6. If the eggs aren't done, microwave for 20 second intervals until they are
