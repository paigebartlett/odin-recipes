The Best Rolled Sugar Cookies

Description

This is a recipe from allrecipe, describing Halloween-themed rolled sugar cookes with icing. I haven't made them myself before but it looks like a fun a delicious recipe to try!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder>
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Then flour, baking power, and salt. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat over to 400 F
  3. Roll out dough, and cut out with cookie cutter. Put cookies 1 inch apart on the cookie sheet
  4. Bake in preheated oven 6 to 8 minutes. Cool on wire rack
