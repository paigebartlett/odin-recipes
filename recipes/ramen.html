No Stove Ramen

A classic recipe for a college student with little money, who is living in the dorms with no stove access. It may no be packed with nutrition, but it makes for cheap and easy calories.

Ingredients

  • Premade Ramen packet, such as Top Ramen
  • A water kettle
  • Heat safe bowl
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Boil water in the water kettle
  2. While water is boiling, put dry ramen into an empty heat safe bowl bowl
  3. Once the water is at a roiling bowl, pour over the noddles until they are fully covered
  4. Mix in flavoring packet
  5. Wait three minutes
